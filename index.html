Privacy Policy for Danger Warning

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Danger Warning ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your full name, email address, and phone number via Firebase Authentication.
  • Location Data: To provide proximity alerts, the App collects precise location data (GPS) even when the app is in the background.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect the descriptions, street names, and timestamps of any hazards or thefts you report.
  • Chat Messages: Messages are stored temporarily and are automatically deleted from our servers 24 hours after being sent.
  • Device Information: We collect device identifiers and usage data to improve app performance and analyze traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: To display reports and calculate user "Levels."
  • Proximity Alerts: To trigger vibration and audio alerts based on your location.
  • Personalization: To display your name on hazard details.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase to store your data securely. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention: Chat messages are automatically purged from our systems every 24 hours.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services: Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase: Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Uses device identifiers to personalize ads.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Safety and Community Standards

Reports are community-generated. The App is not a replacement for emergency services. In a life-threatening situation, always contact local law enforcement first.

7. Data Deletion

Users have the right to delete their reports via the "Profile" screen. For full account deletion, contact: saferidecoding@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically.
