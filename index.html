Privacy Policy for Danger Warning

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Danger Warning ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, and phone number collected via Firebase Authentication.
  • Location Data: Precise location data (GPS) collected to provide real-time proximity alerts, even when the app is in the background.
  • User-Generated Content: Descriptions, street names, and timestamps of hazards or reports you submit.
  • Chat Messages: Temporarily stored and automatically deleted from our servers 24 hours after being sent.
  • Device Information: Device identifiers and usage data to improve app performance and analyze traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: To display reports and track user activity levels.
  • Proximity Alerts: To trigger local notifications based on your proximity to reported hazards.
  • Personalization: To display your identity on content you contribute to the community.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely via Google Firebase. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Chat messages are automatically purged from our systems every 24 hours.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services & Firebase: Used for analytics, app performance, and infrastructure. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to serve ads. AdMob collects advertising identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) to measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and provide personalized advertising content.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Safety and Community Standards

Reports are community-generated. The App is not a replacement for emergency services. In a life-threatening situation, always contact local law enforcement first.

7. Data Deletion

Users may delete individual reports and profile directly via the "Profile" screen. To request the permanent deletion of your entire account, profile, and all associated activity logs, please email: saferidecoding@gmail.com. We will process these requests within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App signifies your acceptance of any updates.
