Privacy Policy for Danger Warning Last Updated: June 6, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how Danger Warning ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. 1. Information We Collect Account Information: When you register, we collect your full name, email address, and phone number via Firebase Authentication. Location Data: To provide proximity alerts, the App collects precise location data (GPS) even when the app is in the background. This is necessary to alert you to hazards within 400 meters of your current position. User-Generated Content: We collect the descriptions, street names, and timestamps of any hazards or thefts you report. These reports are linked to your User ID. Chat Messages: We collect chat messages sent between users. These messages are stored temporarily and are automatically deleted from our servers 24 hours after being sent. Device Information: We collect device identifiers and usage data to improve app performance, troubleshoot issues, and analyze app traffic. 2. How We Use Your Information Core Functionality: To display your reports on the map for other users and calculate your "Level" based on contributions. Proximity Alerts: To compare your live location against reported hazards to trigger vibration and audio alerts. Personalization: To display your name (as "Reported By") on hazard details to ensure community accountability. 3. Data Storage and Security We use Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication) to store your data securely. Your data is protected by industry-standard encryption. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Data Retention: We only retain personal information for as long as is necessary to provide our services. Specifically, chat messages are automatically purged from our systems every 24 hours. 4. Third-Party Services The App utilizes the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you: Google Play Services: Google Privacy Policy Firebase (Analytics/Authentication): Firebase Privacy Policy Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads. 5. Children's Privacy Our Services are not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. 6. Safety and Community Standards By using Danger Warning, you acknowledge that: Reports are community-generated. We do not guarantee 100% accuracy of all pins. The App is not a replacement for emergency services. In a life-threatening situation, always contact local law enforcement first. 7. Data Deletion Users have the right to delete their reports at any time via the "Profile" screen. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data, please contact us at saferidecoding@gmail.com 8. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
